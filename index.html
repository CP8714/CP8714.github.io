The Lichtenburg Woodburning Machine


A Lichtenberg machine is an electrical device that is used by artists, woodworkers, and hobbyists to burn fractal patterns into wood. This fractal burning is achieved by placing high voltage probes across the wood which arc and conduct through the surface causing unique burn patterns. Our design will create these intricate burn patterns but with lower risks to the user through clever and redundant safety features implemented in the design as well as following safety precautions and procedures.
